Arabica vs Robusta


Credits: Pixabay



by: Nikola Borzova


Even though they may seem the same, they are not. Arabica and Robusta are both coffee types. They have different taste, quality, region and specific characteristics.



Firstly let´s talk about Arabica. Origin is from Ethiopia and it´s made from the beans of Coffea Arabica plants. Arabica is more popular then Robusta and you can also get 100% Arabica in every store or coffee place. Arabica´s beans are more popular and required comparing to Robusta and they are also full of antioxidants.
Robusta is grown primary in Africa and it´s way cheaper, because it´s easier to produce. Even though Arabica is more popular, there is 40% of production of Robusta worldwide.


Credits: Pixabay


Difference First of all, there are botanical Arabica contains less caffeine and more sugar then Robusta. Most people have statement, that Arabica has better pleasant taste compare to Robusta. If we talk about caffeine, Robusta contains twice as much. You can also tell the difference in taste, smell and colours. Arabica´s shape is more like oval and Robusta is more rounder. Arabica´s beans are way lighter than Robusta.

Taste Arabica tends to have more sweeter taste and you can also feel chocolate or berries in the background. But the acidity is higher comparing to Robusta. On the other hand Robusta is way more stronger in taste with lower acidity, but higher bitterness. Also Robusta is highly recommended for serving with different kind of drinks, because of the crema and well-marked taste.
